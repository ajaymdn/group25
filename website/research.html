Research

Our research help understand the current state of Motion Input as well as the issues that would arise from using it, as well as how we could go about implementing it within our work.

Current State-of-the-art Approach

Before proposing our own approach to solving the problem, we want to take a moment to summarize influential and state-of-the-art research in this area in the hope that it may better inform our next steps. Thus, we have chosen 8 papers, which we deemed as the most informative for the discipline of Motion Input for gaming.

  • Challenges with implementing gesture detection
    • Has to be efficient enough to be happening in real-time. They advise having just a specific zone, where you are looking for a specific gesture and not looking for it in the whole image.
    • Blending with the background. This can be caused by either inappropriate background OR lack of testing for some skin colours. Both of these should be something that we want to account something in our solution.
